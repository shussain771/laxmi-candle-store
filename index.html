Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell at wholesale rates?
Yes. Wholesale and bulk orders are our specialty. We cater to manufacturers, retailers, SHGs and bulk buyers at competitive wholesale prices.
Do you supply all candle making materials?
Yes. We supply a complete range — paraffin wax, dice wax, wick thread, candle colours, moulds, packaging materials, sealing machines and fragrance oils.
Can beginners buy materials here?
Absolutely. We assist first-time buyers and new entrepreneurs. We can guide you on what materials you need to start your candle-making business.
How do I get the latest price list?
Send us a message on WhatsApp and we'll share the current wholesale price list. Prices are updated regularly based on market rates.
Where is your store located?
We are located at Batasa Patty, M.G. Bazar area, Agartala, Tripura. You can visit us during business hours or contact us on WhatsApp before visiting.
Is there a minimum order quantity?
Minimum quantities vary by product. Wholesale pricing applies from standard bulk quantities. Contact us on WhatsApp and we'll help you plan your order.
